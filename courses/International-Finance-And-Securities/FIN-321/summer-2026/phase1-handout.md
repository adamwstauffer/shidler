## Goal

Using [`_templates/template-decision-memo.md`](../../projects/fx-hedging/_templates/template-decision-memo.md),
write a **300–400 word memo to your CFO** explaining your firm's **FX receivable exposure** and
why hedging is worth considering. The CFO has 90 seconds — executive tone, no filler.

## Your scenario

Use your **assigned scenario** from
[`scenarios.md`](../../projects/fx-hedging/scenarios.md) (sent to you individually). Your firm
expects a foreign-currency receivable on a future date; exchange-rate moves could change how
much you ultimately receive in USD.

## What to include

1. **What the exposure is** — currency, amount, timing.
2. **Why it's risky** — what could go wrong for USD proceeds.
3. **Three hedge families** — forward, money market, options — with honest quick pros/cons.
4. **Next steps** — how you'll build it across Phases 2–5 (spec → AI build + audit → live data →
   validation & recommendation), framed as a plan the CFO can approve.

## Deliverable

- File: `docs/decisions/YYYY-MM-DD-{lastname}-{scenario-slug}-hedge-framing.md`
- One page, from the decision-memo template, YAML frontmatter intact. Committed and pushed.

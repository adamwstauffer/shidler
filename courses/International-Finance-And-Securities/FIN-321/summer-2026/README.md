## Your scenario

Every student is assigned **one** of the four FX scenarios in
[`../../projects/fx-hedging/scenarios.md`](../../projects/fx-hedging/scenarios.md)
(`solar-importer`, `pharma-exporter`, `tech-services`, `aerospace`). Your individual
assignment is sent to you directly (Lamaku / email) — it is **not** posted here. Use your
assigned scenario's parameters as your placeholder inputs from Phase 1 onward; you replace them
with live market data in Phase 4.

## How to submit

- **Phase 0:** submit your **repository URL** via Lamaku.
- **Phases 1–5:** "submit" means **commit and push** to your repository, then submit the
  link to the committed file (or the repo) via Lamaku. Version control *is* the submission
  mechanism from here on.

Use the filename convention `YYYY-MM-DD-{lastname}-{scenario-slug}-{kind}.{ext}` throughout.

# `deliverables/`

Final, presentation-ready outputs (Stage 5). This is the polished layer — what you'd hand to a manager, professor, audit partner, or client.

## What belongs here

- Final PDF reports
- Slide decks (`.pptx` and exported `.pdf`)
- Executive summary memos that synthesize across stages
- Anything explicitly graded as a "final deliverable"

Working drafts and intermediate documents stay in `docs/`. Only move them here once they're final.

## Naming conventions

- `<project>-<deliverable-type>-<YYYY-MM-DD>.<ext>` — e.g., `vingroup-final-report-2026-05-09.pdf`
- Keep the dated filename even after revision — overwrite within a calendar day, but bump the date for any version that changes the takeaways
